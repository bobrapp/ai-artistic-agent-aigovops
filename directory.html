📍 Local Directory

Kittitas County Arts Directory

Galleries, artists, organizations, events, creative businesses, and resources across Ellensburg, Cle Elum, Roslyn, and Thorp. Your guide to the local arts ecosystem.

🖼️

Galleries & Exhibition Spaces 8

Gallery One Visual Arts Center

📍 408 N Pearl St, Ellensburg
The heart of Kittitas County's art scene. Nonprofit gallery with Main, Mezzanine, Eveleth Green, Community, and Hallway galleries. Workshops in clay, watercolor, weaving, sketching. Art After School program. Free admission. First Friday openings.
NonprofitClassesFree AdmissionFirst Fridays
gallery-one.org →

Clymer Museum of Art

📍 416 N Pearl St, Ellensburg
Dedicated to the work of John Ford Clymer (1907–1989), celebrated illustrator and painter of the American West. Permanent collection plus rotating exhibitions. Adjacent to Gallery One on Pearl Street.
MuseumWestern ArtPermanent Collection

PUNCH Projects — Fire House Gallery

📍 10630 N Thorp Hwy, Thorp
Rural arts collective bridging urban and rural art communities. Converted firehouse gallery with rotating exhibitions, music events, and artist collaborations. Open Saturdays 12–5pm. Crew: Justin Beckman, Howard Barlow, Justin Gibbens, Renee Adams, Will Bow.
CollectiveRural ArtsMusic Venue
punchprojects.org →

CWU Sarah Spurgeon Gallery

📍 Randall Hall, CWU Campus, Ellensburg
Central Washington University's primary exhibition space. Faculty shows, MFA thesis exhibitions, visiting artist installations, and student work. Free and open to the public during academic year.
UniversityFreeContemporary

Dick and Jane's Spot

📍 101 N Pearl St, Ellensburg
Iconic outdoor art environment and reflector art installation by artists John Villahermosa and Jane Orleman. Evolving public art landmark — a must-see destination for visitors and locals alike.
Public ArtInstallationOutdoor
reflectorart.com →

Ellensburg Public Library Gallery

📍 209 N Ruby St, Ellensburg
Rotating exhibitions by local artists in the library's gallery space. Free. A quiet, accessible venue for emerging artists to show work.
FreeEmerging Artists

Roslyn Mural Walk

📍 Downtown Roslyn
Collection of outdoor murals throughout historic Roslyn, reflecting the town's coal mining heritage and diverse cultural history. Self-guided walking tour.
Public ArtMuralsHistoric

Local Coffee Shops & Restaurants

📍 Various — Ellensburg & Cle Elum
Several cafes and restaurants display and sell local artwork on a rotating basis, including D&M Coffee, The Yellow Church Cafe, and others. Contact venues directly for exhibition opportunities.
InformalRotating Shows

Events & Festivals 8

First Friday Art Walk

📍 Downtown Ellensburg
Monthly art walk on the first Friday of each month. Gallery One hosts opening receptions (5–7pm). Multiple downtown businesses participate with displays, live music, and refreshments.
MonthlyYear-roundFree

Buskers in the Burg

📍 Downtown Ellensburg
Summer street performance festival organized by the Ellensburg Downtown Association. Musicians, performers, and artists along Pearl Street. Vendor application required.
SummerPerformanceVendors

Kittitas County Fair

📍 Kittitas County Fairgrounds, Ellensburg
Labor Day weekend. Art exhibitions, craft vendor booths, rodeo, livestock, and community events. Competitive art categories with awards. Major annual event drawing visitors county-wide.
Labor DayAnnualVendors

Ellensburg Farmers Market

📍 Downtown Ellensburg
Saturday mornings, May through October. Artisans and makers can apply for vendor booths alongside produce vendors. Sales tax collection required for tangible art.
WeeklySummerVendor Booths

Gallery One Holiday Show

📍 Gallery One, Ellensburg
Annual holiday art sale and exhibition. One of the best selling opportunities for local artists. Application/submission deadline typically September–October.
November–DecemberSalesAnnual

CWU Student & Faculty Exhibitions

📍 Sarah Spurgeon Gallery, CWU
Regular exhibitions throughout the academic year including BFA/MFA thesis shows, faculty exhibitions, and visiting artist installations. Free and open to the public.
Academic YearFree

PUNCH Projects Events

📍 Fire House Gallery, Thorp
Rotating exhibitions, music events, and creative happenings at the converted firehouse. Check their events calendar for current programming. Open Saturdays.
SaturdaysMusicArt

Holiday Craft Markets

📍 Various — Ellensburg & Cle Elum
November–December craft fairs at community centers, churches, and venues throughout the county. Good entry point for makers and craftspeople to sell directly.
November–DecemberCraftSales

Creative Businesses & Services 6

Solarity Credit Union

📍 Ellensburg & Cle Elum
Local credit union recommended for artist banking. Small business accounts, personal loans, and community-focused financial services. Multiple branches in the county.
BankingSmall Business

Suncadia Resort

📍 Cle Elum
Major commercial buyer for original art and prints. Hotels, restaurants, and common areas regularly acquire local artwork. Contact their purchasing department for commission opportunities.
Commercial BuyerCommissionsHospitality

Art Supply — Regional Sources

📍 Online + Seattle
No dedicated art supply store in Kittitas County. Nearest: Artist & Craftsman Supply (Seattle), Blick Art Materials (online). CWU bookstore carries some supplies. Walmart and craft stores carry basics.
SuppliesOnline

Local Framers & Print Shops

📍 Ellensburg
Custom framing and print services available locally. Contact the Arts Commission for current recommendations. For large-format printing, some artists use Seattle services or online providers.
FramingPrinting

SCORE Mentoring

📍 Virtual (nearest chapter: Yakima/Tri-Cities)
Free SBA-funded business mentoring. 10,000+ volunteer mentors nationwide. Not artist-specific, but useful for business planning, marketing strategy, and financial management basics.
FreeMentoringSBA

WA Small Business Development Center

📍 Virtual + Regional Offices
Free business advising for Washington entrepreneurs. Workshops on business plans, financing, and marketing. Not arts-specific but strong on general small business fundamentals.
FreeState Program
