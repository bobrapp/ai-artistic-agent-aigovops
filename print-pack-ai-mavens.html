AI Mavens — Print Pack

AIgovops Foundation · Brand System v9.3

This page produces three letter-size sheets ready for production. Use your browser's print dialog (⌘P on Mac, Ctrl+P on Windows) and Save as PDF to export.

Page 1 — T-Shirt Artwork

Full-scale artwork ready to email to your t-shirt printer. Includes color callouts and print specs. Send as the PDF, or grab the source PNG at assets/ai-mavens/v9/print/ai-mavens-tshirt-print.png.

Page 2 — Sticker Cut Sheet

Six 2.75″ round stickers on letter-size paper with crop marks. Print on full-sheet sticker paper (Avery 22566 or equivalent), cut along the crop marks with scissors or a circular punch.

Page 3 — Business Card Cut Sheet

Ten 3.5″ × 2″ standard US business cards in a 2 × 5 grid. Print on 80-100lb cardstock or use Avery 8371 perforated cards (no cutting needed). Dashed lines mark interior cuts; solid corner marks frame the sheet.

Browser Print Settings

  • Paper size: Letter (8.5″ × 11″)
  • Margins: None (the page sets its own)
  • Background graphics: On (so the navy bar and color callouts print)
  • Headers and footers: Off
