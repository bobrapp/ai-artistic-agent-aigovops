# Kittitas Artist Business Coach

**An AI-powered business advisor for every working artist in Kittitas County, WA.**

A project of the Kittitas County Arts Commission.

## What is this?

The Kittitas Artist Business Coach is a free, 24/7 AI-powered advisor that helps artists in Kittitas County navigate the business side of their practice — taxes, contracts, pricing, grants, insurance, galleries, marketing, and more.

### Three ways artists use it:

- **Ask** — Get answers to any business question in plain English, with Washington state and Kittitas County specifics.
- **Learn** — Walk through any of 15 structured curriculum modules as a guided conversation.
- **Connect** — Get referred to vetted local CPAs, attorneys, grant programs, and arts organizations.

## Living Curriculum

15 modules covering every business topic an artist faces:

| # | Module | Topics |
|---|--------|--------|
| 01 | Hobby or Business? | IRS criteria, Schedule C threshold |
| 02 | Business Structure | Sole prop vs LLC vs S-Corp |
| 03 | WA Registration | UBI, reseller permit, DOR setup |
| 04 | Pricing Your Work | Cost-plus, market comparison, price ladders |
| 05 | Bookkeeping | Wave/QuickBooks, receipt systems, categories |
| 06 | Federal Taxes | Schedule C, estimated quarterly, deductions |
| 07 | WA State Taxes | B&O tax, sales tax, destination-based rules |
| 08 | Contracts | Commission, consignment, licensing templates |
| 09 | IP & Copyright | Registration, fair use, licensing |
| 10 | Portfolio Website | Squarespace, domain, SEO basics |
| 11 | Email & Instagram | Newsletter setup, content pillars, local tactics |
| 12 | Galleries & Consignment | Tiers, contracts, approaching galleries |
| 13 | Selling Online | Etsy vs own store, shipping, marketplace math |
| 14 | Art Fairs & Festivals | Booth economics, inventory mix, local fairs |
| 15 | Health & Retirement | Insurance, Roth IRA, disability, emergency fund |

## Economics

- **Total operating cost:** ~$50/month (not per artist — total)
- **Available:** 24/7/365
- **Capacity:** Unlimited — serves every artist in the county simultaneously
