The Solution

A personal business coach for every artist in the county.

The Kittitas Artist Business Coach is an AI-powered advisor trained on Washington state law, Kittitas County resources, and real artist business needs. Three ways artists can use it:

  • Ask — Get answers to any business question in plain English. Taxes, contracts, pricing, grants, insurance. Real WA-specific guidance, not generic internet advice.
  • Learn — Walk through any of 15 structured curriculum modules as a guided conversation. Like a workshop, but at your own pace, whenever you need it.
  • Connect — Get referred to vetted local CPAs, attorneys, grant programs, and arts organizations when the question needs a licensed human.

Responsible Design

Built-in safeguards.

  • Not a replacement for professionals. The coach never gives legal or tax advice — it educates, then refers to licensed CPAs and attorneys.
  • Escalation triggers in every module. The system knows when to stop talking and hand off to a human expert.
  • Disclaimers are conversational, not buried. Artists are told clearly: "I'm an AI coach, not a CPA. Here's what I can help with, and here's when you need a professional."
  • Quarterly human review. Arts Commission staff review the curriculum and referral list every quarter to keep information current.
  • Local oversight. The system is managed by the Arts Commission — not a tech company. The community controls the tool.
  • Privacy-first. No artist data is sold or shared. Conversations are private.

  • Community — Peer artists answer common questions. The AI handles novel, complex ones. Load drops 30–50%.
