🛡️ AI Protection Framework

This AI Fights For Artists, Not Against Them

The Kittitas Artist Business Coach doesn't generate art, compete with artists, or use artist work as training data. It's a business advisor — and it actively helps artists protect their work from AI companies that do.

"The best way to earn artists' trust with AI is to use AI to protect their rights."

Artists' fear of AI is real — and it's focused on a specific problem: generative AI companies scraping artwork without consent or compensation. The Kittitas Artist Business Coach addresses this head-on, not by dismissing the concern, but by giving artists tools to fight back. The coach becomes an active defender across four layers: protect, detect, fight, and advocate.

Layer 1: Protect — Stop Scraping Before It Happens

The coach walks artists through every available protection tool, step by step.

Glaze Free Coach-Assisted

Applies imperceptible pixel-level perturbations that cause AI models to learn the wrong style from your images. 6+ million downloads since 2023. Run it on every image before posting to social media, your website, or online galleries.
What the coach does: Walks you through downloading, installing, and running Glaze. Reminds you to re-Glaze when updates ship (protection is an arms race — the LightShed bypass published in 2025 means staying current matters).

Nightshade Free

The "offensive" counterpart to Glaze. Poisons training data so that AI models that scrape your images learn corrupted information. From the same University of Chicago lab. 1.6+ million downloads.
What the coach does: Explains the difference between Glaze (defensive) and Nightshade (offensive), helps you choose the right tool for each use case, and walks through the process.

robots.txt AI Crawler Blocks Free Coach Generates

Block AI companies from scraping your website. The coach generates the exact robots.txt file you need, blocking GPTBot, ClaudeBot, CCBot, Google-Extended, and other known AI crawlers.
What the coach does: Generates your robots.txt file instantly. If you use Squarespace, WordPress, or Wix, provides platform-specific instructions. Also generates the TDMRep file (/.well-known/tdmrep.json) required for EU opt-out protection.

C2PA Content Credentials Free

Cryptographic proof of authorship embedded directly in your image files. Created by Adobe's Content Authenticity Initiative. Records who made the image, when, and with what tools — in a tamper-evident format verifiable at verify.contentauthenticity.org.
What the coach does: Explains how to embed credentials using Adobe's free tools or the open-source c2patool CLI. Helps you verify your credentials are working. Notes the limitation: most social platforms strip metadata, so credentials work best on your own website and direct file sharing.

Cloudflare AI Bot Blocking Free New 2025

As of July 2025, Cloudflare defaults to blocking AI crawlers for new sites and added a "Pay Per Crawl" marketplace. If your website uses Cloudflare (free plan available), AI bots are blocked automatically.
What the coach does: Checks if your website already uses Cloudflare. If not, walks you through the free setup. Explains the Pay Per Crawl option — AI companies would have to pay you to scrape your work.

Cara Free

Artist-run social platform that's anti-AI by default. Blocks AI-generated submissions and integrates Glaze for automatic upload-time cloaking. A safe space to share work without scraping risk.
What the coach does: Recommends Cara as a complement to Instagram — share work on both, but know that Cara actively protects you while Meta does not (US artists have no opt-out from Meta's AI training).

Layer 2: Detect — Find Out If Your Work Was Used

The coach helps you search for your work in known AI training datasets.

Have I Been Trained Free Coach-Assisted

Search the LAION-5B dataset (the open dataset behind Stable Diffusion) for your images. Upload your artwork and see if it appears in training data. Also lets you register opt-outs — Stability AI has committed to honoring them for future models. ~80 million artworks flagged by artists so far.
What the coach does: Walks you through the search process. If your work is found, immediately helps you register opt-outs. Then guides you through the next steps (copyright registration for legal standing, connecting with active lawsuits if applicable).

Spawning AI Free

The parent platform behind Have I Been Trained. Provides an API for bulk opt-out registration and tools for platforms to implement opt-out at scale. If you have a large portfolio, Spawning's bulk tools are faster.
What the coach does: For artists with 50+ works, helps set up bulk submission through Spawning rather than one-by-one through Have I Been Trained.

Layer 3: Fight — Take Action When Violations Happen

The coach generates legal documents and connects you to active litigation.

US Copyright Registration $45–65 Coach-Assisted

The single most important step any artist can take. Registration is required to file a lawsuit in US federal court and enables statutory damages up to $150,000 per infringed work. Group registration for unpublished works dramatically reduces per-piece cost.
What the coach does: Generates a photography checklist for documenting your work to registration standards. Walks you through the online registration process at copyright.gov. Helps you use group registration to batch works and save money. Sets calendar reminders for new works.

DMCA Takedown Notices Free Coach Generates

If an AI model produces output that closely reproduces your specific registered work, you can file a DMCA takedown. The coach generates the complete notice, identifies the platform's designated DMCA agent, and walks you through filing.
What the coach does: Generates a legally compliant DMCA takedown notice customized to your situation. Important caveat: DMCA protects against reproduction of specific works, not style imitation. The coach is honest about what's legally actionable and what isn't.

Connect to Active Lawsuits Free

Multiple class actions are actively fighting for artist rights. The coach tracks their status and helps you determine if you're eligible to join.

Active Lawsuits the Coach Tracks

Bartz v. Anthropic — $1.5 Billion Settlement

Settled October 2025. The largest AI training data settlement in history. Class covered up to 500,000 works at ~$3,000/work. Anthropic agreed to destroy the pirated datasets. Book authors, but set the precedent that training on scraped copyrighted material is actionable.

Andersen v. Stability AI, Midjourney, DeviantArt, Runway

Class action by visual artists. Direct copyright infringement claims survived motions. In active discovery. This is the case that will define visual artist rights against AI. No trial date set.

Getty Images v. Stability AI (UK)

Judgment November 2025. Trademark infringement claims (generating images with Getty watermarks) survived. The UK case is narrower but established that AI-generated outputs containing copyrighted elements are infringing.

NYT v. OpenAI

Not an artist case, but the most-watched test of fair use for AI training. Motion to dismiss denied March 2025. Discovery ongoing. The outcome will affect all creators.

Layer 4: Advocate — Stay Informed, Stay Organized

The coach keeps you connected to the movement protecting artist rights.

Legislation Alerts Proactive Push

The coach monitors federal and Washington State legislation affecting artist rights and pushes timely alerts.
Current tracking:
• TRAIN Act (federal, Aug 2025) — Would give copyright holders the right to access AI training datasets and sue for unauthorized use. Bipartisan.
• NO FAKES Act (S.1367, Apr 2025) — Creates property rights in voice and visual likeness against digital replicas.
• CLEAR Act (federal) — Would require disclosure when copyrighted work is used in AI training. Endorsed by Graphic Artists Guild.
• WA HB 1168 — Would require AI developers to document training data sources. Passed committee but stalled by tech lobby pressure. The coach alerts you when it's revived and tells you who to contact.

Organization Connections Free
