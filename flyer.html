Example conversation
You
Do I need to collect sales tax if I sell pottery at Buskers in the Burg?
KC
Yes — Washington requires you to collect sales tax at the event location's rate. For Buskers in the Burg, that's Ellensburg's rate of about 8.2%. The event organizer may issue a sub-permit that handles some of this — I'd recommend confirming with the Ellensburg Downtown Association in writing before the event. You'll need your WA UBI number and reseller permit. Want me to walk you through the full sales tax module?
You
Wait, what's a UBI number?
KC
Your Unified Business Identifier — it's Washington's version of a business registration number. You get one when you register with the Department of Revenue. It takes about 15 minutes online and there's no fee. Let me walk you through it step by step.
