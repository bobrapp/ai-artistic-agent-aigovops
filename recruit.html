Subject: Free AI protection workshop + business coaching for Kittitas County artists
From: Dr. Don Solberg, Kittitas County Arts Commission
To: [Artist / Gallery / Org]

Hi [First Name],

Did you know that if you've ever posted artwork on Instagram, your website, or Etsy, it may already be in an AI training dataset — scraped without your permission or compensation?

This isn't hypothetical. Stable Diffusion was trained on over 5.8 billion images pulled from the open web. Midjourney won't even disclose its training data. And Meta recently started using every public Instagram post to train its AI models — with no opt-out for US artists.

The good news: there are real tools you can use right now to protect your work. And they're free.

We built something to help.

The Kittitas Artist Business Coach is a free, AI-powered advisor built specifically for working artists in our county. It's available 24/7 and covers everything from taxes and pricing to contracts and gallery consignment.

But here's what makes it different from generic business advice: it actively helps you fight back against AI companies scraping your work.

The coach can walk you through:

  • Glaze — free software that cloaks your images so AI can't learn your style (6 million+ downloads)
  • Copyright registration — how to protect up to 750 works for $85, unlocking the right to sue for up to $150,000 per infringed work
  • AI opt-outs — company-by-company, which ones actually work and which don't
  • DMCA takedowns — step-by-step, including generating the notice for you
  • robots.txt blocking — stop AI crawlers from scraping your website in 10 minutes

Total cost for full protection: under $100 and about 2 hours of your time.

It also handles the business stuff that keeps you up at night.

  • WA taxes explained in plain English — B&O, sales tax, when to collect, how to file
  • Pricing help — cost-plus, market-based, and value-based frameworks for your specific medium
  • Contract templates — commission agreements, gallery consignment, licensing deals
  • Local knowledge — Gallery One terms, CWU opportunities, Suncadia wholesale, First Friday logistics
  • Grant guidance — Artist Trust, ArtsWA, NEA, and what your application actually needs

We're looking for 25 pilot artists.

We're inviting a small group of Kittitas County artists — all mediums, all experience levels — to test the coach for 90 days starting this summer. It's completely free. You'll help us make it better, and you'll get genuine business help in the process.

Interested? It takes 2 minutes.

  • This is not an AI art generator. It doesn't create images, doesn't use your work, and doesn't compete with you. It's a business advisor — think "AI that helps with your taxes," not "AI that paints your paintings."
  • It was built by the Arts Commission specifically for our community. The local knowledge is real — Ellensburg sales tax rates, Gallery One's consignment terms, Kittitas County Fair deadlines.
  • Everything you share with the coach stays private. We don't sell data, period.
