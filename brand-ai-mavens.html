The Character

AI-Yes.

A human-and-silicon hybrid hero. The face, head, and arms are human; the torso, hips, legs, and cape are chrome silicon armor. The cybernetic seam at the neck and the shoulder pauldrons where the human arms emerge are where the partnership reads.

The human is a beautifully multi-ethnic person of color of blended East Asian, South Asian, Native American, and Anglo heritage, with a graceful blend of feminine and masculine features — a singular fusion person embodying the unity of humanity itself. The doubled hybridity (human-blends-human, then human-blends-silicon) makes the character a literal embodiment of the foundation's whole story.

The green YES emblem at the chest is the brand's beating heart — a single spot color in an otherwise blue-and-grey duotone. When you read the design, your eye lands there first. That's intentional. The foundation says yes.

Usage

How to ship it.

Production notes for the print shop, the merch store, and anyone applying the system.

T-Shirt Print

Heather grey or charcoal cotton tee. Chest placement, centered. Spot-color screen print preferred — six screens (paper, steel, slate, navy, electric blue, green). DTG acceptable for short runs.

Logo Application

Use the badge at any size ≥ 18mm for the foundation plaque to remain legible. Maintain 0.5× badge-radius of clear space on all sides. Avoid placing on busy backgrounds.

Sticker Production

Die-cut vinyl, 75mm diameter recommended. White outer border 3mm wide. UV-resistant ink for outdoor use. Designed to survive on laptop lids and water bottles.

Business Card

Premium 350gsm matte cardstock, 85×55mm or US 2×3.5in. Logo at 55-60% card width, centered upper-third. Foundation name + URL in Inter 8pt below.
